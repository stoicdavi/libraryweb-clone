MyLOFT

MyLOFT is an off-campus software that facilitates seamless and secure access to subscribed eResources. Library users can discover, organize ,share save and sync eContent between PC and Mobile with a sign click from any website or mobile app

Features Of MyLOFT

  • Access to subscribed eResources
  • Offline reading
  • Tag and Organize
  • Highlight and Listen (TTS)
